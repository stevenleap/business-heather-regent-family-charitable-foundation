About the Foundation

Purpose & record

Heather Regent Family Charitable Foundation was registered as a charitable organization effective February 17, 2024. Our purpose is to receive and maintain a fund or funds and to apply all or part of the principal and income therefrom from time to time to charitable organizations that are also registered charities under the Income Tax Act (Canada). The charity was inactive during its first year as it was waiting for the initial contribution to be received in 2025.

  • Registered charity with a clear purpose to support other registered charities.
  • Transparent operations with public records available.
  • Focused on deliberate, long-term charitable giving.

Based in Toronto, ON, Canada. We support registered charities across Canada.
